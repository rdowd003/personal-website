Services

Two ways in.

Whether you need a strategic partner in the room or a builder who can go hands-on, I work across both — as a leader, an advisor, or a fractional resource for healthcare and digital health teams.

Track 01

Strategy & Advisory

For leaders who need clarity on where data and AI create real value — and a concrete plan to get there.

What this looks like

  • Data & AI strategy — use-case prioritization, build-vs-buy, and ROI frameworks
  • Operational strategy & org design for data and analytics functions
  • KPI & metric frameworks that align teams on what actually matters
  • Fractional data / analytics leadership
  • Digital health & real-world evidence strategy
Best for founders, operators, and executive teams.
Track 02

Technical & Hands-On

For teams that need someone who can actually build it — not just advise on it.

What this looks like

  • Data science projects — modeling, forecasting, risk & segmentation
  • Data engineering & warehouse / pipeline architecture
  • Analytics layer & BI design (Looker, dashboards, metric stores)
  • Data quality, monitoring & governance systems
  • AI/ML proof-of-concept design and evaluation
Best for teams needing senior delivery, freelance or embedded.

How I work

Diagnose before prescribing.

01

Diagnose

Understand the real problem — often the one no one has fully named yet — before proposing anything.

02

03
